All Skins are made to be shown with their armor being invisible, and were meant to be played as a casual way!
This can get disadvantaging for those who plays mini-games or anything more competitive!

What you can do if you still want to use it for competitive play and that you want to use armor on Miku/Teto skins :

1 - Go to PCK Studio.
2 - Select MikuModules.pck or TetoVariants.pck
3 - Select your desired skin in the pack
4 - Double-click on "ANIM" under "Properties"
5 - Disable "Disable All Armor" on the skin, then save.
